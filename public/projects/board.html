Rules:

  • 0, 1: Tile dies
  • 2, 3, 4: Tile survives
  • 5, 6, 7, 8: Tile dies
  • Don't let your opponent see the tiles you place.
  • Death is calculated when both players have placed their pieces
  • If both players place a tile in the same location, the tiles are used but the space remains blank
  • Each player starts with 6 tiles to place, and can place as many as they want each turn
  • Each player gains 2 tiles/cycle, up to a max of 6
  • Winner is the last one with living tiles
